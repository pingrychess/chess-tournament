Tournament Rules

  • All matches follow standard chess rules (FIDE).
  • Each player has a 10-minute clock for their moves.
  • Results must be reported within 24 hours of match completion.
  • Players can challenge opponents up to 5 ranks above them.
  • The top 8 players will advance to playoffs for a single-elimination showdown.
